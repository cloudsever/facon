Tam Coc Sunset Homestay: A Haven of Peace and Sustainability

In the heart of Ninh Binh, Vietnam, lies Tam Coc Sunset Homestay, a sustainable aparthotel that promises a unique blend of tranquility, comfort, and eco-friendly living. This homestay, located 23 km from Bai Dinh Temple and 31 km from Phat Diem Cathedral, is a perfect retreat for those looking to explore the natural beauty and cultural heritage of Vietnam.

Embracing Sustainability

Tam Coc Sunset Homestay is committed to sustainability, having implemented 26 out of 29 sustainable measures. This commitment is evident in every aspect of the homestay, from waste recycling to the use of energy-efficient lighting. The homestay's dedication to environmental stewardship ensures that your stay not only comforts but also contributes positively to the planet.

Comfortable and Eco-Friendly Accommodations

The homestay offers air-conditioned accommodations with amenities designed for comfort and convenience. Each unit includes:

  • Free WiFi with a speed of 44 Mbps, suitable for streaming and video calls.
  • A terrace, mountain views, and a seating area for relaxation.
  • Satellite flat-screen TV, a fully equipped kitchen with a fridge and minibar.
  • A private bathroom with a walk-in shower and slippers.
  • A patio with an outdoor dining area and garden views.

Amenities for a Memorable Stay

Tam Coc Sunset Homestay provides a range of facilities to enhance your experience:

  • A snack bar and a minimarket for your daily needs.
  • Facilities for guests with children, including a baby safety gate.
  • Bicycle and car rental services to explore the surrounding areas.
  • A paid airport shuttle service for convenient transportation.

Exploring the Local Area

The homestay's location is ideal for those wishing to immerse themselves in the local culture and natural beauty of Ninh Binh. It is conveniently located 7 km from Ninh Binh Stadium and 8.9 km from Thung Nham Bird Park Ecotourism. The peaceful setting outside Tam Coc is perfect for those seeking a serene escape, with easy access to local attractions.

Activities for Every Interest

Tam Coc Sunset Homestay caters to a variety of interests:

  • Walking and bike tours to explore the local landscape and culture.
  • Proximity to key attractions like Cuc Phuong National Park and the ancient capital of Hoa Lu.
  • A variety of dining options, including family dinners, to savor local flavors.

Guest Experiences: A Reflection of Excellence

With an impressive rating of 8.8, guests consistently praise the homestay for its exceptional service, cleanliness, comfort, and value for money. The tranquil location, quality of meals, and the availability of bikes for exploration are particularly appreciated by guests.

Why Choose Tam Coc Sunset Homestay?

  • Sustainable Practices: A commitment to eco-friendly living with various sustainable measures.
  • Comfort with a Conscience: Well-equipped rooms with modern amenities, set in a sustainable environment.
  • Ideal Location: Close to cultural and natural attractions, offering a mix of peace and exploration.
  • Personalized Service: Warm hospitality and services tailored to meet guest needs.

In summary, Tam Coc Sunset Homestay is more than just accommodation; it's a destination that offers a harmonious blend of sustainable living, comfort, cultural immersion, and adventure. Whether you're a solo traveler, with family, or a couple, this homestay promises an enriching and unforgettable experience in the beautiful Ninh Binh province of Vietnam.
