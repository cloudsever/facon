Tam Coc Sunset Homestay: Escape to Tranquility

Nestled in the heart of Ninh Binh, Tam Coc Sunset Homestay is a sustainable apart hotel that beckons travelers with its picturesque views and warm hospitality. This hidden gem is located just 23 km away from the majestic Bai Dinh Temple, offering a perfect blend of convenience and tranquility. As you step into this serene abode, you'll be greeted by a lush garden and breathtaking city views, setting the tone for a memorable stay.

Embracing Sustainability and Comfort

Tam Coc Sunset Homestay is more than just a place to rest; it's a commitment to sustainability. The air-conditioned accommodations are designed with eco-friendliness in mind, providing a comfortable retreat for eco-conscious travelers. The aparthotel boasts not only modern amenities but also a conscious effort to reduce its environmental footprint.

Key Features:

  • Free WiFi: Stay connected with friends and family while immersing yourself in the beauty of Ninh Binh.
  • Free Private Parking: Hassle-free parking ensures that your exploration of the region is seamless.
  • Facilities for Disabled Guests: Tam Coc Sunset Homestay welcomes all guests, ensuring that everyone can enjoy its enchanting ambiance.

Your Home Away from Home

Upon entering your cozy retreat, you'll be greeted with a sense of home. Each unit at the aparthotel offers a private terrace, providing breathtaking mountain views. The interiors are thoughtfully designed, featuring a comfortable seating area, satellite flat-screen TV, and a fully equipped kitchen with a fridge and minibar. The private bathroom, complete with a walk-in shower and slippers, adds a touch of luxury to your stay.

In-Room Amenities:

  • Patio with Outdoor Dining Area: Enjoy a meal amidst nature with a private patio and garden views.
  • Quality Bed Linen and Towels: Revel in the comfort of carefully chosen bed linen and towels during your stay.

Culinary Delights and Convenience

Savor the flavors of Ninh Binh without leaving the homestay premises. Tam Coc Sunset Homestay offers a snack bar, providing a quick culinary fix for guests. Additionally, a minimarket on-site ensures that your basic needs are conveniently met, enhancing the overall comfort of your stay.

Family-Friendly Features

Traveling with children? Tam Coc Sunset Homestay goes the extra mile to ensure a family-friendly environment. The aparthotel provides a baby safety gate, offering peace of mind for parents. Create lasting memories with your loved ones as you explore the nearby attractions together.

Explore Ninh Binh with Ease
